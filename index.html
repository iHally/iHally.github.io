Functional Capacity Assessments

Because life isn’t one-size-fits-all, and neither is your support plan

We take a deep dive into your everyday life: what’s working, what’s tricky, and what supports could make a real difference. We’ll chat together (and with anyone else you’d like involved), observe how things are going at home or in the community, and carefully consider each part of your life: valued roles, mobility, self-care, communication, social connections, decision-making, and more.

Our goal is to paint a clear picture of your support needs and strengths, so your NDIS plan is shaped around you.

NDIS Pricing: Typically delivered over 15 hours, including assessment, report writing and liaison.

Supported independent living

Your life, your home, your supports - let’s get it all working together!

Thinking about living more independently: on your own or with housemates? I can help figure out what level of support you might need and what that could look like day-to-day.

I’ll get to know your routines, your preferences, and any challenges that come up around living skills like cooking, cleaning, staying safe, managing health, and community access. I can talk with your support team, gather evidence, and put together a clear, person-centred recommendation.

Whether you’re new to SIL or reviewing an existing arrangement, this will help your application reflect the support that really suits your life.

NDIS Pricing: Typically delivered over 20 hours, including interviews, observations, report writing, and collaboration with your provider.

Specialist Disability Accommodation

Home should fit - not just physically, but in every way that matters

Some people need more than standard housing; they need a home that’s specifically designed to suit their support needs. That’s where SDA comes in.

I’ll work with you to understand your current living situation, the barriers you face, and what kind of housing could help you live with greater safety, dignity and independence. This involves a detailed assessment, liaison with your therapy team, and a strong report that outlines how and why SDA can help. Whether you’re applying for the first time or updating your evidence, I’ll support you every step of the way.

NDIS Pricing: Typically delivered over 20 hours, including assessment, team liaison, housing exploration, and report writing.
